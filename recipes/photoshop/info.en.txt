Adobe Photoshop CC 2021 (offline installer)

Requirements:
• Proton-GE (used automatically)
• Adobe offline installer in: photoshop/ (Set-up.exe + products/)
• About 15 GB free disk space
• Installation is silent (no Adobe GUI, no winecfg)

After installation:
• Use “Launch” in the launcher or the desktop shortcut
• First start may take 1–2 minutes
• Blurry fonts: Launcher → “Install” again (repair) or check the log

Logs: ~/.local/share/wine-software/logs/
